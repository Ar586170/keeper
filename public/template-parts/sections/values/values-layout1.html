OUR VALUES
Here are the four guidelines we stick to
protect you in these shifting environments.

We have a
strong passion.

The boundaries between "work" and "play" aren't always
obvious. We are all connected and motivated by our
curiosity and creativity. Staying in your comfort zone
is never a good idea in our opinion.

We leave egos
at the door.

We challenge ideas and offer candid criticism, but we also
emphasize the points in which we agree. We will challenge
things in order to produce better work, but never merely
out of curiosity. There isn't time for that.

We enjoy
learning.

We enjoy learning, particularly from one another. We are
always improving our abilities in design, development, and
copywriting as well as learning how to use new platforms
and features. Because lifelong learners.

We support
one another.

Our days are spent constructing sites and strengthening
one another. An opportunity to interact, work together, and
recognize our clients and coworkers arises with each new
project. We're in this together and for the long haul.
